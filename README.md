# Fashion Design Academy (Moda Dizayni Akademiyasi)

## English

Fashion Design Academy is an interactive, web-based learning platform for students who want to study fashion design in a structured and practical way.

The course is divided into 8 progressive stages:

- Foundations of Fashion Design
- Figure Drawing & Illustration
- Color Theory & Psychology
- Pattern Making Basics
- Draping & Fit
- Advanced Construction Techniques
- Print, Motif & Collection Development
- Branding, Business & Market Strategy

Each stage includes:

- Structured lessons with theory and visual examples.
- Interactive games to practice key concepts.
- Quizzes to test understanding.
- A glossary system that tracks important terms you have learned.

As you complete each stage, the next one unlocks automatically. When all stages are finished, you receive a digital certificate confirming your completion of the program.

By going through this academy, a student will:

- Build a solid theoretical foundation in fashion design.
- Develop practical skills in illustration, pattern making, draping, and construction.
- Learn how to create cohesive collections using color, prints, and motifs.
- Understand branding, market positioning, and business basics for fashion designers.
- Be ready to continue into advanced study or real-world projects with confidence.

## O‘zbek tilida

Moda Dizayni Akademiyasi — bu moda dizaynini tizimli va amaliy yo‘lda o‘rganmoqchi bo‘lgan talabalar uchun interaktiv, veb-platforma asosidagi ta’lim loyihasi.

Kurs 8 bosqichga bo‘lingan:

- Moda dizayn asoslari
- Figura chizish va illyustratsiya
- Ranglar nazariyasi va psixologiyasi
- Konstruksiya (kroki) asoslari
- Drapirovka va moslash (fit)
- Yuqori darajadagi tikuv texnikalari
- Print, motiv va kolleksiya yaratish
- Brending, biznes va bozor strategiyasi

Har bir bosqichda quyidagilar mavjud:

- Nazariy va vizual misollar bilan tuzilgan darslar.
- Asosiy tushunchalarni mustahkamlash uchun interaktiv o‘yinlar.
- Bilimni tekshirish uchun test savollari (quiz).
- O‘rganilgan atamalar bo‘yicha lug‘at tizimi.

Har bir bosqichni yakunlaganingizda, keyingi bosqich avtomatik ravishda ochiladi. Barcha bosqichlar tugagach, siz dasturni muvaffaqiyatli tamomlaganligingizni tasdiqlovchi raqamli sertifikat olasiz.

Ushbu akademiya orqali talaba:

- Moda dizayn bo‘yicha mustahkam nazariy asos hosil qiladi.
- Illyustratsiya, kroki chizish, drapirovka va tikuvda amaliy ko‘nikmalarni rivojlantiradi.
- Ranglar, printlar va motivlar yordamida uyg‘un kolleksiyalar yaratishni o‘rganadi.
- Brending, bozorda joylashish va moda dizaynerlari uchun biznes asoslarini tushunadi.
- Keyingi darajadagi ta’limga yoki real loyihalarga ishonch bilan kirishga tayyor bo‘ladi.
